Emotional Intelligence

Emotional intelligence includes mindfulness and self knowledge. Emotional intelligence and EQ can be just a important as IQ. It is also a charachteristic of good leaders. The difference between EQ and IQ is that EQ monitors emotional intelligence whilst IQ monitors intellectual intelligence.
