Identity Values

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.
When I had a role being a support representative in finance there were times when I had the ability to give financial advice to customers however this was not in the scope of a support representative. Also you needed specific qualifations to provide this financial advice. Customers would still ask for finanacial advice and whilst at times I thought I could provide them with a decent answer I had to make the ethical decision and tell customers that I am not able to provide this advice. With time I reflect on this as making the correct decision.
Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.
My family and friends have shown me great values and I use them as examples to help my adjust when needed.
Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.
I think I am quite a good learner if the material provided is sufficient and I am able to ask questions if required and I think this will help me in my learning.
Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?
It can take time for me to learn concepts if I do not intuitively understand them. In cases like these I will need to spend more focused time on these concepts.
Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.
In University there were many group projects and in a few cases not every person in the team provided a sufficient effort. In times like these I would have discussions with them and ask if they needed help and if the could still contribute which was effective. Relfecting on this I would have offered my help earlier.
